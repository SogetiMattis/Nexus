    SAP runs the business processes in Equinor

    SAP is the system of record for the business processes in Equinor, such as purchasing, materials management, HR to Finance & Control. SAP is also a system of innovation - a development platform and set of tools to support adaption of the system and the development of loosely coupled custom solutions.
